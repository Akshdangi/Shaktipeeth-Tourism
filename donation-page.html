Why Donate?

Your generous contribution helps maintain the sanctity of India's most revered Shakti Peethas — temples that hold centuries of devotion, history, and spiritual energy. Every donation directly supports:

  • 🛕
    Temple Restoration — Preserving ancient architecture and sacred structures for future generations
  • 🍚
    Annadaan (Free Meals) — Providing free food to thousands of pilgrims daily at temple premises
  • 🪔
    Daily Puja & Rituals — Sustaining sacred ceremonies, aarti, and festivals throughout the year
  • 📚
    Spiritual Education — Funding Vedic schools and cultural programs for local communities
  • 🏗️
    Pilgrim Infrastructure — Building shelters, rest houses, and clean water facilities for devotees
🛡️
All donations are tax-deductible under Section 80G. Receipts are sent via email immediately.
